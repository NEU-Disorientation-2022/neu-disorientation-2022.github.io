The following PDF contains excerpts from a "Roxbury Development 101" booklet, which was a crash course on local development processes made by Alternatives for Community and Environment and Reclaim Roxbury. Alternatives for Community and Environment is a neighborhood-based environmental-justice and transit-oriented development nonprofit which has been defending the rights of Roxbury residents for over 25 years, and Reclaim Roxbury is a broad-based community effort dedicated to improving the quality of life and economic wealth of all Roxbury residents.
